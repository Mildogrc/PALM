About

From Conceiving to Deploying

  • August 2018

    Our Humble Beginnings

    MyPALM was first created when our a group of friends decided that it was time for a change

  • December 2018

    An Agency is Born

    MyPALM started in the production faze. The team started collecting massive amounts of data(~1TB or about 2 million images) and started the training of a legendary model: “PALM” This model is now the most advanced model in the world and actually stands for Pneumonia Artificial Learning Model.

  • March 2019

    Transition to Full Service

    The team decided it was time to move away from creating the model and move on to actually helping people with it. They went to local hospitals to show them this model and asked how they can use it to help people in need.

  • by 2020

    To The Future

    The team hopes that before 2020 that this model will at least be used within the county that they reside. Although this may seem like a small step, it is one of many that will lead to revolutionizing the world.

MyPALM is a technology created from our team, and it has the ability to take images of x-rays and figure out if that image has Pneumonia

MyPALM is based off PALMnet, our secret Neural Network through which every images passses through. It is extremely accurate and uses convolutional Neural Networks. It also has all the power of Google's Resnetv50, since PALMnet was created using transfer learning with Resnet as the base model.
